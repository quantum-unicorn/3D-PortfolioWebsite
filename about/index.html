About me

Perhaps as the result of never having gaming hardware or of having played with children’s programming languages from a young age, I was always more intrigued by game development than playing games themselves.

I first picked up Blender around 2013 and used it on and off until the 2.80 update in 2019. That’s when I started doing ArchViz and modelling as volunteer and commission work, honing my skills and familiarizing myself with most areas of the 3D pipeline.

  • Areas I’m most comfortable with:
    • 3D modelling
    • Texturing
    • Procedural materials
  • Areas I’m familiar with:
    • Digital sculpting
    • Photogrammetry
    • 2D image manipulation
  • I have basic knowledge of:
    • Rigging
    • Animation
    • Programming
    • Environment design

I am a GNU/Linux and open source software enthusiast so most of my skills are in tools like Blender, GIMP and Godot game engine. However, I am confident enough in my knowledge to say I’d be able to translate these skills to other software in good time.
